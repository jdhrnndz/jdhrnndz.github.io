Regulus SpaceTech Inc. is a pioneering space technology company in the Philippines that focuses on space research and development, education and consultancy services. Our mission is to help improve the lives of ordinary Filipinos through utilization of space technology and to promote space education and awareness throughout the country. We specialize in astronomy education, astrophysical instrumentation and observation, nano-satellite development, and advanced instrumentation for various scientific and commercial applications.
